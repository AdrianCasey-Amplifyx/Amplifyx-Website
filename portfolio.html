Viden Advisory

Process AI - End-to-End AI Product Development

The Client

Viden Advisory, an accounting advisory firm building a proprietary AI-powered accounts payable product.

Our Role

As Product Lead, I manage all R&D initiatives and lead end-to-end product, software, and AI development for Process AI - from strategy through to production deployment.

What We've Built

Process AI is an intelligent accounts payable automation platform that transforms how finance teams handle invoices:

  • AI-powered OCR extracts invoice details and line items from uploaded documents or forwarded emails
  • AI orchestrated workflows ensure invoices are correctly classified with general ledger codes, tracking categories, and tax rates
  • Intelligent supplier matching validates ABN details and maps to existing Xero contacts
  • Fraud prevention checks including bank detail verification and calculation validation
  • Custom admin portal enabling organisation admins to self-manage users, sync Chart of Accounts, and monitor integration health
  • Supervisor agent for handling complex line item queries and adjustments
  • Direct Xero integration for seamless push of approved invoices

The Impact

Organisations process invoices in minutes instead of hours, with AI handling classification and data entry while humans focus on review, exceptions, and fraud prevention.
